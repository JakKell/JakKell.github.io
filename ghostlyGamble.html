Ghostly Gamble

A senior project developed by Jacob Kelley

Ghostly Gamble is a 1-vs-1 card game where you compete against a CPU to gain points. You can play honestly to gain guaranteed points or lie to try and get ahead. You can make your opponent discard cards and even steal their points to steal the game. Can you beat the CPU on its highest difficulty?

The visuals are intended to look like a casino with a spooky ghost design.

Try and guess whether the CPU is lying or telling the truth.

Play up to four cards per turn.

Use effects to set your opponent back.

Play again and again or adjust the win condition and CPU difficulty.
