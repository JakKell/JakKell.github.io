School and Professional

My name is Jacob Kelley. I am a soon to be graduate of Southern Illinois University in Carbondale. I will be graduating with a major in computer science and a minor in math. I have also accomplished specializations for artificial intelligence and machine learning as well as cybersecurity within my major. I also hope to be graduating with a near 4.0 GPA. I am the president of my school’s computer science club, ACM, which focuses on developing computer science skills for students. We have participated in multiple hackathons, the most recent of which we earned honorable mentions for our groups pixel art color by number generator. I am also the treasurer of Techdawgs, a community outreach organization focused on teaching the community technology. We have created and conducted several successful and well rated activities, such as a python turtle workshop. I also volunteer weekly at the Saluki Food Pantry located at SIU’s student center. I have had the opportunity to intern two summers at State Farm and I plan on working there as a software engineer after I graduate.
